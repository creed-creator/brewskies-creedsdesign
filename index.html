Our Story

Your Downtown
Neighborhood Spot

Brewskies Downtown is the go-to gathering place in the heart of Seymour. Whether you're coming in for a game, a family dinner, or just good wings with good people — this is your place.

We serve everything from hand-breaded tenderloins and fresh burgers to pizzas, wraps, shrimp baskets, and homemade sides. Cold beer on tap, craft cocktails, and a menu that keeps people coming back.
